Our Story

The story of how we met begins by a microwave in the basement of the Economics Department at the University of Toronto. On a fated August day in 2023, as James waited for his lunch to warm up, Olivia spotted an opportunity to recruit another player for Graduate Econ Friday Soccer. It was the early days of graduate school: Olivia had just started the second year of her PhD, and James had just begun his MFE.

We were friends first, hanging out over lunch between classes and playing board games with their friends over the weekend. But eventually, James fell in love and confessed his feelings over coffee on a Tuesday afternoon in November. It took Olivia a week to agree to go on a date with James, but from there, our friendship turned into something neither of us could imagine living without.

Getting There

Getting to Toronto

We recommend flying into Billy Bishop (YTZ) if possible, just minutes from downtown Toronto.
Otherwise, the UP Express train gets you from Pearson (YYZ) to Union Station in 28 minutes.

Car / Rideshare

Uber, Lyft, and taxi services are available throughout Toronto.
Municipal Green P Parking lots and spaces are available near both venues.

Subway

Take TTC Line 1 to Dupont Station and enjoy a short walk past the Archives Library and up the Baldwin Steps to Spadina House.
Comfortable shoes are recommended, and this route may not be ideal for guests with mobility concerns.

Streetcar

The 504A King to Distillery streetcar connects King Station (TTC Line 1) to the Distillery District.
